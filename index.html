HOW IT WORKS

Three steps. One score. Before the damage is done.

Sentinel AI works silently in the background — no training required, no manual checking.

1

You receive a message

Sentinel AI monitors your conversations from WhatsApp, Instagram, Facebook, Telegram, and SMS — with your permission. Only your score is ever stored. Never the content.

2

In seconds, Sentinel AI assesses the conversation. Powered by a proprietary AI agent that never stops learning — continuously trained on emerging fraud methods so it stays ahead, not behind.

Based on the risk score, Sentinel AI's agent takes action — from a quiet cleared tag to a full conversation lock. Calibrated to protect you without interrupting your life.
